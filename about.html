Meet the Maker

Hi! I’m B! An earring enthusiast with a strong passion for colour and crafts. All my earrings are individually designed, moulded and assembled by hand. I’m a Graphic Designer by trade with a degree I recently completed in May 2020, but I’ve always had a passion for creating things by hand.

I have launched madebyb earrings, as a way to keep my hands busy this lockdown. I aim to build this dream into a sustainable business, creating limitless designs and applying my graphic design skills where possible. I will continue to create handmade polymer earrings until one of you lot tell me to stop.

I hope you continue on this journey with me!

Love B xox

How B's earrings are made:

Designing:

All earrings are sketched up on paper to get an idea of size and shape.

Sculpting:

The clay colour is selected, rolled out, printed and cut before baking.

Baking:

The earrings go in the oven for 30 minutes at 150C, and are then left to cool. We know they have been cured properly when we can bend them without snapping!

Adding findings:

Lastly, I drill holes for the findings and pop them together ready to be shipped off to you!

Delivered to you:


All earrings are carefully wrapped up in tissue paper and packaged in a lightweight box or padded envelope. I aim to dispatch your order 7 days after transaction takes place. I will try my very hardest to ensure every item is sent off in a timely manner, but since I am a one man show and my earrings are made to order I prefer not to rush the process.

*Due to COVID-19, delivery times may vary.
